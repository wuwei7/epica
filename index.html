Welcome to the Epica Fan Page

This is a fan page about the Dutch symphonic metal band Epica. Formed in 2002 by guitarist, vocalist and songwriter Mark Jansen, the band plays symphonic metal with gothic and death metal influences (among many others). They are known for their use of opera choirs and orchestras both in studio and live performances. They have released eight studio albums and have toured the world multiple times.
